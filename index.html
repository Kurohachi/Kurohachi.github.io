If you do not possess any of the documents, please provide both of the following.

  • Health insurance card
  • National health insurance card
  • Japanese passport
  • Basic resident registration card
  • Certificate of physical disability + Supplementary documentation
  • Certificate of residence
  • Notification of evacuation site certificate
  • Utility bill or receipt for electricity, gas, or water

*Supporting documents are required if your address is not specified in the document.
*Rakuten Mobile will never store your insurance number or card code/number (QR codes).
*No additional supporting documents are required if your name, address, and date of birth are printed or handwritten with an official seal.
*Please bring original documents (not copies) that match the applicant's name and address and have been issued within the last 3 months. If the name on the documentation does not match the applicant's name, they are ineligible to apply.
